What is StudentSuccessHub?

Student Success Hub is a space for university students who want to get the most out of their studies and life outside class. We provide practical tips on meal prepping, planning, self-care, and hobbies that help make student life more manageable and productive.

Through relatable advice and shared experiences, we help students stay organized, maintain energy, and balance responsibilities without feeling overwhelmed. Student Success Hub is here to offer guidance, inspiration, and useful strategies so you can navigate university with confidence and a little more ease.

Web Designer / Developer

Hi, my name is Larayb Raja. I am an aspiring digital design student studying at Roehampton University for my second year. My website, Student Success Hub, is the outcome of a project that we were assigned in class. I created this website based around motivating and helping both current and potential university students to balance their studies and personal lives more effectively.

I have shared many different tools on this site from recipes, to planners, to encouraging stories from former students all in the hope of it resulting in the user becoming more happy with their student life.
